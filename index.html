Who We Are

A builder defined
by what we deliver.

Columnpic is a Canadian construction company headquartered in Ottawa, Ontario. We are focused on one thing: building exceptional homes for Canadian families and landowners.

We hold ourselves to standards that go beyond code. Every home we deliver is engineered for durability, energy performance, and long-term value — because we believe a home is the most important investment a family will make.

We work with a select number of clients at a time. This is intentional. It allows us to give every project the attention it deserves.

Our Method

Factory-Built.
Site-Fit.

Columnpic homes are not built in the open — exposed to weather delays, scheduling uncertainty, and the variability that defines traditional site construction. Every component is precision-engineered in a controlled manufacturing environment, quality-verified to exact specification, then delivered and assembled on your property.

This is a fundamentally different way to build a home — and a better one. Factory conditions eliminate the inconsistencies that compromise traditional builds. The result is a tighter envelope, superior energy performance, and a quality of finish that open-site construction cannot reliably replicate.

Our clients receive a finished home. Not a construction site.

Precision
